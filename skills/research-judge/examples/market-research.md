# Example: Market Research

Prompt:

```text
Use the research-judge skill to evaluate whether a local adult learning service marketplace can work in Zhuhai.
Separate source-backed facts from assumptions, map conflicts, and give a decision-oriented brief.
```

Expected behavior:

- Do not jump straight to “yes” or “no.”
- Identify the actual buyer and user.
- Separate market size claims from local demand proof.
- Compare adult learners, college students, parents, teachers, and local institutions.
- Map conflicts such as high demand vs low willingness to pay, fragmented supply vs trust problem, and online alternatives vs local service value.
- Recommend the smallest validation test.
